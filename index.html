Get to Know Me

Engineering Autonomy with Purpose

Hi, I’m Harshavarthan Varatharajan — a Robotics Software Engineer who builds autonomous systems end-to-end, from perception and motion planning to reliable, real-time control. I ship clean ROS 2/C++ and Python code and care deeply about turning ideas into robots that actually work in the real world.

Recently, I’ve worked on CAN-FD control, motion planning and retargetting for dexterous hand/arm teleoperation, terrain-adaptive locomotion research (CROSS-GAiT), and simulation pipelines in MuJoCo/Gazebo. I enjoy designing planners with MoveIt 2/OMPL, integrating depth & vision (RealSense, SLAM), and closing the loop on hardware with robust testing and tooling.

Beyond the stack, I value clear docs, thoughtful operator UX, and fast, iterative teams. If you’re building intelligent machines and need someone who blends AI, control, and systems engineering — let’s talk.
